# Brand Campaign Rules

## How Brands Evaluate Creators

Key metrics brands look for:
- **Engagement rate**: Quality of audience interaction, not just follower count
- **Audience alignment**: Does the creator's niche match the brand's target?
- **Content consistency**: Regular posting and consistent quality
- **Authenticity**: Genuine voice and audience trust

## Creator Tiers

Industry-standard categorization:
- **Nano**: 1K-10K followers (highest engagement rates, local reach)
- **Micro**: 10K-100K followers (niche authority, strong community)
- **Mid-tier**: 100K-500K followers (broader reach, established voice)
- **Macro**: 500K-1M followers (mainstream appeal)
- **Mega**: 1M+ followers (celebrity status, mass awareness)

## What Brands Pay For

Value drivers in partnerships:
- Engagement rate over follower count
- Niche relevance and audience demographics
- Content production quality
- Exclusivity and usage rights
- Multi-platform presence

## Red Flags for Brands

Things that decrease brand interest:
- Inconsistent posting schedule
- Low engagement relative to follower count
- Generic content without clear niche
- Fake followers or engagement
- Controversial history

## Partnership Rates

General guidelines (vary by niche/market):
- Nano: $50-$250 per post
- Micro: $250-$1,000 per post
- Mid-tier: $1,000-$5,000 per post
- Macro: $5,000-$25,000 per post
