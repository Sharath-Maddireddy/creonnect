# Instagram Best Practices (2025)

## Algorithm Fundamentals

The Instagram algorithm prioritizes content based on:
- **Engagement velocity**: How quickly a post receives interactions after publishing
- **Watch time**: For Reels, completion rate and replays are critical signals
- **Saves and shares**: Higher-weight signals compared to likes
- **Comments**: Especially meaningful conversations (not just emojis)

## View-Based Metrics

Modern Instagram success is measured by:
- **Engagement rate by views**: (likes + comments) / views × 100
- This matters more than follower-based engagement
- A 5-10% engagement rate by views is considered excellent
- Below 2% indicates content not resonating with viewers

## Content Discovery

How content reaches new audiences:
- Reels are shown on Explore and Reels tab to non-followers
- Hashtags help with categorization, not primary discovery
- Consistent posting builds algorithmic favor
- Audio trends can boost Reel visibility

## Posting Strategy

Best practices for growth:
- Post consistently (3-5 times per week minimum)
- Engage with comments within first hour
- Use Stories to boost main feed content
- Analyze which content types perform best
