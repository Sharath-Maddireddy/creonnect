# Creator Growth Playbook

## Foundation: Niche Selection

Successful growth starts with:
- Clear niche positioning (fitness, tech, lifestyle, etc.)
- Understanding your target audience deeply
- Consistency in content themes and style
- Differentiation from other creators in your space

## Growth Strategies by Stage

### Early Stage (0-1K followers)
- Post frequently to test what works
- Engage heavily with similar creators
- Focus on quality over viral attempts
- Build genuine connections

### Growth Stage (1K-10K)
- Double down on best-performing content types
- Collaborate with creators at similar level
- Start building email list or community
- Experiment with Reels if not already

### Scale Stage (10K-100K)
- Optimize posting schedule based on data
- Consider brand partnerships
- Build additional revenue streams
- Hire help if needed (editing, management)

## Content Optimization

What drives algorithm favor:
- Strong hooks in first 1-3 seconds
- Clear value proposition in every post
- Consistent visual style/branding
- Captions that encourage engagement

## Engagement Best Practices

Building real community:
- Reply to comments within first hour
- Ask questions in captions
- Use polls and questions in Stories
- Create shareable/saveable content

## Common Growth Killers

Mistakes that stall growth:
- Inconsistent posting schedule
- Ignoring analytics and insights
- Chasing trends without brand alignment
- Buying followers or engagement
- Neglecting community building
